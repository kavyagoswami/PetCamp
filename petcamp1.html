Pet Camp Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 18/09/2023

Pet Camp's("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our non-profit organization, including through our website, events, and other activities. By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
1. We may collect the following types of personal information:
1.1. Contact Information: This may include your name, email address, postal address, and phone number.
1.2. Donation Information: If you choose to donate to our organization, we may collect financial information, such as credit card or bank account details
1.3. Website and Usage Information: We collect information about your interactions with our website.
1.4. Communications: We may store records of your communications with us, including emails and messages sent through our website or social media channels.
1.5. Volunteer and Event Information: If you volunteer for or attend our events, we may collect information related to your participation,
such as event registration details and volunteer preferences.

2. We use your personal information for the following purposes:
2.1. To Provide Services: We use your information to deliver the services you request.
2.2. To Improve Our Services: We analyze data to improve our programs, website, and communications with our supporters.
2.3. To Communicate: We may send you updates, newsletters, and other information about our organization, events, and initiatives.

3. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.
However, we may share your information with:
3.1. Service Providers: We may engage third-party service providers to help us with website hosting, payment processing, and other
operational needs. These providers are required to protect your information.
3.2. Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.

4. We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.
However, please be aware that no data transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

5. You have the right to: 5.1. Access and Update Your Information: You can review and update your personal information by contacting us.
5.2. Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving our communications at any time by following the instructions in our emails or contacting us.

By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms and conditions.
