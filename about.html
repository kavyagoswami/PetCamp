About Us



Our Mission

Our biggest mission is to provide safe place to the animal who is needs itand not only
to the abandoned animals, the strays too. Talking about strays, if they're sick or suffering
from any diseases we'll bring them to our shelter on one phone call. We are trying make
India cruelty free.



Adoption

For adoption, if there is any queries or want to adopt a pet please vist us or give us a call.
We have few animals in our shelter or we can even arrange for you. All the medical formalities
will be done by us, your only responsibility is to give them Love and Care. The only thing you
have to do is to provide your correct identity so we can know that the pets are safe with you.
